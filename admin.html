Recent alerts

Tour-wide settings — set these up once per tour, then press Publish to push changes live. The app works out the live day automatically from your schedule dates, so it always opens on the right day while you’re away.

Tour

Front page image

The badge shown on the app’s welcome screen. Upload a square-ish logo or photo (a PNG with a see-through background looks best). Large images are shrunk automatically. It goes live when you press Publish.

Weather location

For anywhere not in the list, enter its coordinates. Easiest way: search the place on Google Maps, right-click the spot, and the latitude & longitude are the two numbers at the top of the menu — copy them in.
Shown to players when they tap the weather bar, above the live 7-day forecast. Refresh this per trip.

Getting around

Players & staff get an “Open in Maps” tile for the team hotel; supporters get one for the supporter hotel (falls back to the team hotel if left blank). Match grounds link to maps automatically from each game’s Location.

Match officials & support

Shown on the Get-help screen under “Match referee” and “Technical support”. These can also be set from the spreadsheet’s Tour Details tab. Leave blank to hide.

Paperwork — forms & links

These appear in the app under “Before you go › Paperwork”. Consent, visa, landing card and handover always show — if a link is blank, players see “Contact TCA Sports Tours if you need this” so they know who to ask.
With a link, players get a button to it; without one they see the “Contact TCA Sports Tours” note. Show or hide it with the Behaviour contract switch under Access & switches › App switches.
Leave any of these blank and the app shows “Contact TCA Sports Tours if you need this” for that item — add the link when you have it.

Shop — kit & merch

A “Shop” tile under “Help & people” for team kit and merch. Leave the link blank to use the default Global Cricket Series shop, or paste a tour-specific URL. Show or hide it with the Shop tile switch under Access & switches › App switches.

Tournament rules

When set, a “Tournament rules” tile appears for everyone under “Help & people”, linking to the playing conditions and format. Leave blank to hide it. Also settable from the spreadsheet (“Tournament Rules Link”).

Excursions & car hire

Optional activities and car hire. These show in the app under “Before you go” to staff and supporters only (not players). Give each one a website link, a phone/WhatsApp number, or both, and an optional date/availability note for limited tours. You can also load these from the spreadsheet’s Excursions tab.

Access

A separate sign-in for scorers and umpires. They get a matches-only view (formats, grounds & map links, kit, ball, umpire/scorer names, both squads with numbers, and the published batting orders) — nothing else. Set a code to restrict it, or leave blank to let anyone choose “Scorer / umpire” on the sign-in screen.

App switches

Every on/off switch in one place. Press Publish after changing.

Players’ Player voting window (optional)

Normally voting opens by itself 24 hours before the final game and closes when that game starts. Set exact times below to override, or use the Force voting open now switch above for testing.
Before this the app shows a live countdown on the schedule and the staff tile; the moment it opens, the app posts an alert to all players and staff.
After this moment voting is firmly closed, even if “Force voting open now” is ticked. Leave blank to use the final-game default.

Teams

Add a row per team — however many you list is how many the tour has. Each team gets its own sign-in and sees only events set to their name or to “Everyone”. Renaming a team here updates it across the whole schedule. Use the photo button on a row to give that team its own logo.
Players (squads) tap to expand · also fills from the spreadsheet
Each team’s squad, used for the bus check-in register, batting order and meal/vote counts. Add players here, or upload them with the spreadsheet later (this same list gets filled). One name per row.
Staff & coaches tap to expand
Contacts shown to each team (in More → Staff & coaches, and on the Get-help screen). Add name and phone per team. Use full international format like +44 7700 900000 so WhatsApp and calling work abroad.
Publish to app sends your changes straight to the live app — players see them within seconds, with no files to move. The buttons below are extras: a backup file, the schedule code, or a spreadsheet.

Extras

All submissions (all teams)

A company-wide view of meal pre-orders across every team. Load it live, read it on screen, then download each restaurant’s orders as a spreadsheet to forward on. (Team coaches only see their own team inside the app; this sees everyone.)

Testing — clear submitted data

Wipes everyone’s submitted entries from the live app so you can re-test from empty. Your schedule, squads, menus, staff and settings are not touched — only what players have sent in. This can’t be undone. Roll calls aren’t here — they’re kept on each staff member’s own phone (a coach can re-open a roll call and tap “Clear all” on their device).
